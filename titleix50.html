ROAD TO 50

Title IX turned 50 on June 23, 2022

Since day one, the Women’s Sports Foundation has been working toward gender equality in sport through research, advocacy, education, grant-making, and leadership development. As of the 50th anniversary, WSF has invested over $100 million, expanding access and opportunities for all girls and women to reach their potential in sport and in life. But key questions remain. How do we educate and empower the next generation of advocates? How do we fulfill the true promise of Title IX? What do the next 50 years look like? Join us as we look back, honor today, and move forward.

TITLE IX

37 words and 50 years
that changed
everything.

“No person in the United States shall, on the basis of sex, be excluded from participation in, be denied the benefits of, or be subjected to discrimination under any education program or activity receiving federal financial assistance.”

Title IX of the Education Amendments Act of 1972

STAY ENGAGED

Signature Activations

WSF is proud and grateful to partner with so many strong, supportive organizations — dedicated to leveling the playing field for all athletes.

VIRTUAL TOWN HALL SERIES

Throughout 2022, WSF hosted a series of free Virtual Town Halls open to the public, featuring leaders, advocates and athletes discussing the progress and promise of Title IX. Topics include youth participation, pay equity, leadership, public policy, LGBTQ+, disability, and media.

50 YEARS OF TITLE IX: WE’RE NOT DONE YET

WSF released a research report that examines the progress made in the first 50 years and looks to the future – what needs to change and what steps need to be taken in order to achieve gender equitable treatment and inclusive excellence in sports.

DEMAND IX

WSF, Billie Jean King Leadership Initiative and National Women’s Law Center formed the Title IX Anniversary Coalition and launched a national campaign to increase the pace of change toward equity and fairness for all.

NGWSD VIRTUAL 5K & 50 MILE CHALLENGE

presented by

DICK’S Sporting Goods

Whether you chose to walk, swim, bike, ski, roll or run all 50 miles at once or spread out your movement throughout the weeks leading up to the Title IX festivities in June, we thank you for taking part in this community-based celebration.

ADVOCACY IS A TEAM SPORT

Activities & Events

WSF is proud to highlight efforts commemorating the importance of Title IX and gender equity in sports.

CURRENT EVENT

All American: The Power of Sports Exhibit

September 16, 2022

National Archives will host the exhibit: All American: The Power of Sports beginning on September 16. WSF and Billie Jean King are on the honorary committee. This event runs until January 2024.

UPCOMING

National Girls & Women in Sports Day® Briefing

February 1, 2023

The Women’s Sports Foundation will host a briefing for congressional members and staffers on Capitol Hill commemorating the 37th Annual National Girls & Women in Sports Day (NGWSD). This discussion will focus on the wellbeing of athletes and efforts for greater gender equity in sports. Speakers will include student-athletes, elite athletes, and women’s sports advocates.

PAST EVENT

New-York Historical Society’s Title IX Exhibit

May 12-September 4, 2022

Open May 12 through September 4, 2022, the New York Historical Society’s Title IX exhibit will include select items from the Women’s Sports Foundation and Billie Jean King Archives.
